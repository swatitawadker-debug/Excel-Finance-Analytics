# Excel-Finance-Analytics

## Project Overview:
This project simulates a real-world business scenario for AtliQ Hardwares, where Finance data are transformed into actionable insights using Excel, Power Query, Power Pivot, and DAX and analyzes the financial performance using Profit & Loss (P&L) reports across fiscal years, months, and markets to uncover key trends in revenue, cost, profitability insights and market performance.

## Project Objective:
   **1.** Create Profit and Loss (P&L) reports by Fiscal Year[https://github.com/swatitawadker-debug/Excel-Finance-Analytics/blob/main/P%20%26%20L%20Statement%20by%20Fiscal%20Year.pdf] = Summarizes yearly financial performance, highlighting strong revenue growth alongside declining profit margins.

   **2.** Create Profit and Loss (P&L) reports by Months[https://github.com/swatitawadker-debug/Excel-Finance-Analytics/blob/main/P%20%26%20L%20Statement%20by%20Months.pdf] = Provides monthly financial trends to analyze seasonality, sales patterns, and performance fluctuations.

   **3.** Create Profit and Loss (P&L) reports by Markets[https://github.com/swatitawadker-debug/Excel-Finance-Analytics/blob/main/P%20%26%20L%20Statement%20by%20Markets.pdf] = Compares market-wise revenue and profitability to identify high-margin and underperforming regions.

   **4.** Create Gross Margin Percentage by Quarters[https://github.com/swatitawadker-debug/Excel-Finance-Analytics/blob/main/GM_Percent_By_Quarters.pdf] = Shows quarterly gross margin trends across regions to track profitability consistency and regional performance differences.

## Insights:

   ### Revenue Growth
       - Sales increased from $87.5M → $196.7M → $598.9M       
       - Huge growth in 2021 shows strong business expansion

   ### Profitability Concern
       - Gross Margin dropped from 41.4% → 36.4%
       - Business is growing, but profits are not increasing at the same rate

   ### Market-Level Insights
      - High-profit markets: New Zealand (~48%), Japan (~46%), UK (~45%)
      - Low-profit markets: Germany (~26%), Norway (~29%)

  ### Monthly Trends
      - Strong seasonal spikes observed (Nov–Dec highest sales) = Opportunity for seasonal campaign optimization
